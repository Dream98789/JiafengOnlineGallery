About Artist Jiafeng

Jiafeng is a renowned artist whose works are diverse and profound. His creative style is unique, not limited to any form or medium, but explores all possibilities. Recently, he has focused on handmade ceramic teapots, with each piece reflecting his passion for art and pursuit of innovation.

Most of the works you see below are handmade with Taiwanese clay and fired at a high temperature of 1250°C, making them durable and timeless. If you love tea culture, Jiafeng's works are definitely worth collecting.
